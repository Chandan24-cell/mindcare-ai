    This application is an AI-assisted stress and mental state analysis tool developed for educational, research, and wellness purposes only.

    Medical Disclaimer

    It is not intended for medical diagnosis or treatment. Results may be probabilistic and affected by environmental conditions, lighting, camera quality, and other factors. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals.

    User Responsibilities

    • Use the application responsibly and ethically
    • Do not rely solely on AI analysis for critical decisions

    Privacy Policy

    Zero Data Storage

    No images, videos, or sensor data are stored on our servers. All processing occurs in real-time and data is immediately discarded after analysis.

    Camera Access

    Webcam access is requested only with explicit user consent. The camera feed is processed locally in your browser whenever possible. No recording or storage occurs.

    No Tracking

    We do not use third-party analytics, tracking cookies, or advertising networks. Your privacy is our priority.
